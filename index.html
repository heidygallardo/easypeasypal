EasyPeasyPal

Our Mission

EasyPeasyPal is dedicated to making technology effortless and accessible for everyone. We recognize the challenges of navigating the digital world, especially for those with limited tech experience or disabilities. Our app bridges this gap with an intuitive interface, transforming technology from a challenge into a trusted, empowering companion. At the heart of EasyPeasyPal are features like appointment management, accessible communication, medication and health assistance. As we evolve, we're committed to enhancing these functionalities, ensuring technology seamlessly integrates into and enriches everyday life for all. Join us in our pursuit of a more inclusive digital future.

Caretaker Role

Click button below to learn more!

EasyPeasyPal introduces a specialized 'Caretaker Role' feature, tailored to facilitate caretakers in supporting their patients or loved ones. This unique role allows caretakers to access and manage the same features available to regular users, but with the added capability of overseeing another user's account. Caretakers can seamlessly schedule appointments, manage medications, and ensure effective communication, all from a single, intuitive interface. This feature underscores our commitment to making digital tools more inclusive and supportive, empowering caretakers to provide better, more coordinated care, while maintaining the dignity and independence of those they assist.

APPOINTMENTS
Role: Users

This feature gives users access to:

  • Create appointments, edit existing appointments, and delete any scheduled appointments.
  • Access a weekly view of their appointments.
  • A monthly view calendar where they are able to navigate to another date quickly.
  • All events button to view all appointments they have scheduled.
COMMUNICATION
Role: Users

This feature gives users access to three main buttons:

  • Chat button: Allows users to see chats they have with their closest circle without having to scroll through Dominoes Happy New Year's text. Once in a chat they are able to send and receive text messages in real-time!

  • Contacts button: Allows users to search for other EasyPeasyPal users by email and add them to their contacts list with a simple click.

  • Help button: Provides explanation of how the Chat and Contacts buttons are used.
MEDICATION
Role: Users

This feature gives users access to:

  • Create medications, edit existing medications, and view all of their medications in one place.
  • Information they can save includes: the medication name, the quantity, how often it is taken, and any intake instructions they should follow when taking the medication.
PERSONAL CARE
Role: Users

This feature gives users access to:

  • Input their weight and height from which their Body Mass Index (BMI) is calculated, allowing users to know where they stand health wise.
  • Know whether their blood pressure is high, low, or normal once they input their blood pressure.
  • Store information for their well being such as: allergies, blood type, and health goals they may have.
